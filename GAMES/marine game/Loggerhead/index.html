Trust Reconstruction

A VR two-player game about rebuilding trust between humans and marine life.

Project Type

VR Game · Two-Player Game

Tools

Unity, Blender

Trust Reconstruction is a two-player VR game that lets one player take the role of the loggerhead sea turtle and the other the human rescuer. Through card-based choices and dual perspectives, players experience the complexity of rebuilding trust: how to discern genuine intentions, how to communicate across species, and how to make decisions that are both forward-thinking and sustainable. Only when sincere hands courageously reach into the sea and experience the living conditions of animals can the turtle's trust eventually be reconstructed, and survival continue in a clean environment.
